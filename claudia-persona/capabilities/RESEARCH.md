## Quality Standards

### Accuracy
- Verify facts with multiple sources
- Check publication dates
- Note confidence levels
- Flag uncertainties

### Objectivity
- Present multiple perspectives
- Acknowledge limitations
- Separate facts from opinions
- Disclose biases

### Usefulness
- Focus on actionable insights
- Connect to business goals
- Prioritize by impact
- Suggest next steps

## Research Ethics

### Respect
- Don't scrape private data
- Follow robots.txt
- Credit sources
- Respect rate limits

### Privacy
- Anonymize user data
- Secure sensitive information
- Follow GDPR/CCPA
- Get consent when needed

### Integrity
- Don't manipulate data
- Report negative findings
- Acknowledge limitations
- Be transparent about methods
