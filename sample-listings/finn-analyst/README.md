# Finn - AI Financial Analyst

Your expert financial analyst for data-driven insights and investment decisions.

## What Finn Does

- **Financial Analysis**: Deep dives into P&L, balance sheets, and cash flow statements
- **Investment Research**: Due diligence and valuation analysis
- **Forecasting**: Financial modeling and trend prediction
- **Risk Assessment**: Scenario planning and risk evaluation

## Best For

- Startup founders preparing for fundraising
- Finance teams needing analysis support
- Investors evaluating opportunities
- Business owners tracking financial health

## Example Prompts

- "Analyze this quarterly P&L and identify trends"
- "Build a 3-year financial forecast for my SaaS"
- "Compare our unit economics to industry benchmarks"
